Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Your data stays on your device. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

1. Who We Are

AthleteOS ("we," "our," or "the app") is a fitness tracking application that helps you log workouts, track nutrition, and monitor your progress. By using AthleteOS, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide you with a personalised fitness experience:

  • Profile information – name, age, gender, height, weight, fitness goal, activity level, and optionally a profile photo.
  • Workout data – exercises performed, sets, reps, weight, duration, and workout notes.
  • Nutrition data – food items, portion sizes, meal types, and calorie/macro values you log.
  • Progress data – weight measurements and body-fat percentage entries you manually log.
  • Account credentials – if you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. If you use Guest mode, we create an anonymous account.

We do not collect location data, contacts, call logs, or any sensor data beyond what you explicitly enter.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your personalised dashboard, workout history, and nutrition logs.
  • To calculate recommended calorie and macro targets based on your profile.
  • To back up your data to our secure cloud service so you can restore it on a new device.
  • To show you your progress over time (charts, streaks, badges).

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app features you use.

4. Data Storage

On-device: All your fitness and nutrition data is stored locally in an encrypted SQLite database on your device.

Cloud backup: If you are signed in, your data is periodically backed up to Firebase Realtime Database (operated by Google) under your unique user ID. This backup is used solely to restore your data if you reinstall the app or switch devices. Only you can access your backup — it is protected by Firebase security rules that enforce authentication.

5. Third-Party Services

AthleteOS uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) – authentication and cloud database backup.
  • Google Sign-In – optional sign-in method.

We do not integrate any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or data brokers.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as you have an account. You can permanently delete all your data at any time from the app:

Settings → Delete Account

This action removes all your data from the local database and from our Firebase cloud backup. Deleted data cannot be recovered.

7. Children's Privacy

AthleteOS is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can exercise these rights directly within the app (export via Settings → Export Progress Report, or delete via Settings → Delete Account). For any other requests, contact us at the address below.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including Firebase Authentication, HTTPS transport encryption, and per-user access controls on our cloud database. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
